What is Hell Week?

Hell Weeks are our first long day practices as the 2017-2018 team! During hell weeks, we condition to build our stamina and strength. These two weeks are challenging but also fun and rewarding! It is important to stay hydrated and eneergized so bring lots of water and healthy snacks!
