Glendale Dance/Drill Team

HALLOWEEN



Ever since 1996, Glendale High School's Dance/Drill Team dress up together as a team every Halloween. Weeks before Halloween, the team picks a specific theme and plans everyone's costumes accordingly. People on the team are given characters that matches their role on the team or personality. What is more amazing about the team's Halloween dress-up is that each costume for the team is made by the team members with the help of Glendale Dance staff.

WE ARE EXCITED TO ANNOUNCE THAT THIS YEAR'S (2017-2018) HALLOWEEN THEME WILL BE HARRY POTTER.
