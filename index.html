Glendale Dance Drill Team

The Dance Team's History

Since 1999, under the direction of Kelly Palmer, the dance/drill team program has won over 50 National Championship titles. The GHS JV and Varsity dance/drill team competes annually at the United Spirit Association Nationals competition. This is held at the Anaheim Convention Center. The GHS dance/drill team consists of more than 80 dancers with ten coaches and a director.

Our Director

Our director has been directing the GHS Dance Drill Team since 1995. She is highly respected by her dancers, her collegues, and throughout the dance community.

Our Coaches

Each of our coaches is a Glendale alumni. They have a deep understanding of the ropes of the team, as well as the ways of the competitive dance circuit.

Our Captains

The captains of the team are members of the team that are elected by their peers and coaches. Each captain is in charge of a specific competition routine or program.
