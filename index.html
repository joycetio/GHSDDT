OUR HISTORY


Since 1999, under the direction of Kelly Palmer, the dance/drill team program has won over 50 National Championship titles.

The GHS JV and Varsity dance/drill team competes annually at the United Spirit Association Nationals competition.

This is held at the Anaheim Convention Center. The GHS dance/drill team consists of more than 80 dancers with ten coaches and a director.
